Step 02

Keep It
Live.

Your website needs a home on the internet — that's hosting. Without hosting, your site doesn't exist. Think of it like rent for the space your website occupies online. It also keeps your site fast, secure, and online 24/7.

A site that goes down loses customers. A site with an expired SSL certificate gets flagged as "Not Secure" by browsers — and people leave. A site with no backups can disappear overnight from one bad update. Hosting isn't optional. It's the foundation your site runs on.

  • No hosting = no website, full stop
  • SSL certificates keep your site secure and trusted by Google
  • Monthly backups mean you never lose your site
  • Uptime monitoring alerts you the moment anything goes wrong

Step 03

Get Found
on Google.

A beautiful website no one sees is a waste of money. SEO — Search Engine Optimization — is what makes Google send people to your site instead of your competitor's. It's the difference between showing up on page 1 and being buried on page 5 where nobody looks.

Local SEO is especially powerful for small businesses. When someone searches "barber near me" or "auto shop in [your city]," Google decides who shows up in those top results and in the map pack. The right SEO setup puts you there. And unlike ads, once you rank — you keep ranking without paying per click.

  • 75% of users never scroll past the first page of Google results
  • Local searches ("near me") drive foot traffic and phone calls
  • SEO compounds over time — the longer you do it, the stronger you rank
  • Google Business Profile alone can double your local visibility
